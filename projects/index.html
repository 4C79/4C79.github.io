Projects

Trajectory of flying target and impact point prediction based on binocular camera

  • The binocular camera system creates a link between the camera and the real world, using EPNP to compute three-dimensional coordinates and drawing on target detection and prediction algorithms to chart the flight path and forecast the drop point of a moving target.
  • Experimental environments for drones are simulated through specific datasets, allowing the assessment of alternative targets within these spaces.

Tracking and motion trajectory analysis of microorganism video sequences

  • Designing a tracking model leveraging deep similarity learning, grounded in the SiamMask and video segmentation, pinpoints rumen protozoa with enhanced precision and interprets their behaviors via tracking paths.
  • Refined with an annotated protozoan dataset, underpinned by algorithmic theory, to boost model accuracy.
  • Transformed the model into user-friendly tracking software using py-installer, leading to software applications and published research.
