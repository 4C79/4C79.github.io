Volunteer

Teaching in Remote Areas under the “Micro Light” in 2023

In 2023, I participated in the “Micro Light” volunteer program, which provides educational support to children in remote areas. My responsibilities included lesson planning and conducting tutoring sessions, where I developed engaging teaching methods to enhance learning. This experience not only earned me the title of Outstanding Volunteer for Teaching in Remote Areas but also deepened my commitment to educational equity and social responsibility.

Volunteer Experience in Campus COVID-19 Prevention in 2022

During the COVID-19 pandemic, I served as a volunteer for campus epidemic prevention efforts. My role involved coordinating activities such as health screenings, distributing safety information, and ensuring compliance with health protocols. This experience enhanced my organizational skills and reinforced my belief in the importance of community support during challenging times.

Rural Living Conditions Survey Volunteer in 2019

As a volunteer in the joint rural living conditions survey in northwest China, I engaged with local communities to understand their living situations and needs. My role involved conducting interviews, collecting data, and participating in activities to promote community development. This experience deepened my awareness of rural challenges and strengthened my commitment to contributing to societal improvement.
